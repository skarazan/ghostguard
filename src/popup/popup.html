GhostGuard

Before you start

GhostGuard reads job listing pages you visit to score them locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device.

LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor's Terms of Service prohibit automated reading of their pages. Using this extension may violate those terms and could result in account restrictions on those platforms. You use this extension at your own risk.

Under US law (hiQ v. LinkedIn, 9th Cir. 2022), reading pages you have legitimate access to is not a federal crime. GhostGuard does not bypass any login or technical control.
