# GhostGuard Privacy Policy

GhostGuard does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on remote servers. Everything stays on your device.

## What we access

We read the content of job listings you view on LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor in order to score them. This data is processed entirely in your browser and never leaves your device.

## What we store

We cache scoring results locally using your browser's storage API so we don't have to re-analyze jobs you've already seen. This cache is automatically cleared after 24 hours and is limited to 5000 entries.

## What we share

Nothing.
